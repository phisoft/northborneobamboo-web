Bamboo in Architecture

Natural Conductor

Known as a natural conductor, absorbing the heat whilst cooling the surrounding environment and is known to withstand the tests of time.

Higher Specific Compressive Strength

Higher specific compressive strength compared to wood, brick and even concrete.

Easy to be found

Bamboo grows easy & we have the good fortune for having an abundance of the plant growing in our backyard.

Best Choice for Wooden Materials

Mostly found in Asia, Australia, some parts of the Americas and sub-Saharan Africa, the plant comes in large varieties, differing according to region.

Availability

Bamboos are the popular choice in wood because of its’ availability, among many other reasons.

Modern Architecture & Technology

The boon of modern technology increased the longevity of bamboo plants with treatments, making it more than suitable for a long-lasting, durable and beautiful home.

❝ With the shake that Sabah experienced in 2015, durability has since come to play as well. Better to be safe than sorry!

Interesting Facts

Each bamboo species will develop flowers at the same time, no matter where they are located in the world.
Bamboo grows in a wide range of environments. It can survive over 120 years in the wild.
Bamboo produce 35% more oxygen compared to an equivalent stand of trees.
